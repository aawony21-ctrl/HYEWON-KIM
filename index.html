ABOUT

Hyewon Kim is a Seoul-based designer working across visual identity, editorial, and motion. Her work often begins with language — a tension between what is said and what is seen — and resolves itself through restraint, repetition, and careful attention to form.

She has collaborated with cultural institutions, independent studios, and emerging brands, approaching each project as a negotiation between concept and material. Nothing is treated as background; everything is deliberate.

Currently available for freelance projects and long-term collaborations. She believes good design is less about visibility and more about clarity — the kind that only shows itself slowly.
